Practice Problem 2.4

Without converting the numbers to decmal or binary, try to solve the following
arithemetic problems, giving the answers in hexadecimal. Hint: Just modify the
methods you use for performing decimal addition and subtraction to use base 16.

A. 0x503c + 0x8 = ________
B. 0x503c - 0x40 = ________
C. 0x503c + 64 = ________
D. 0x50ea - 0x503c = ________


Answer:

A. 0x503c + 0x8 = 0x5044
B. 0x503c - 0x40 = 0x4ffc
C. 0x503c + 64 = 0x507c
D. 0x50ea - 0x503c = 0xae
