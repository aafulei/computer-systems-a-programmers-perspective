Practice Problem 2.1

Perform the following number conversions:

A. 0x39A7F8 to binary
B. binary 1100100101111011 to hexademical
C. 0xD5E4C to binary
D. binary 1001101110011110110101 to hexademical

Answer:

(A)
0x     3    9    A    7    F    8
0b  0011 1001 1010 0111 1111 1000

(B)
0b  1100 1001 0111 1011
0x     C    9    7    B

(C)
0x     D    5    E    4    C
0b  1101 0101 1110 0100 1100

(D)
0b    10 0110 1110 0111 1011 0101
0x     2    6    E    7    B    5
